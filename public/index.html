How it works

Three steps to a fully autonomous AI phone agent

01
📲

Get a DID

We provision a carrier-grade phone number (DID) on the platform. SMS + voice + voicemail — all wired up and ready.

02
🧠

Connect your AI pod

Your baby-claw pod spins up — a lightweight OpenClaw instance configured for your use case. Pure AI reasoning, zero telecom complexity.

03
🚀

Go live

Your number is live. Inbound calls, texts, and voicemails flow to your AI pod via the ClawCell broker. It responds, decides, and acts — automatically.

Features

Everything your AI needs to be on call 24/7

📱

SMS · Voice · Voicemail

Full telecom stack handled by the platform. Your pod receives normalized events — no carrier SDKs, no SIP stacks, no ffmpeg. Pure AI reasoning.

Telnyx Twilio fallback Whisper STT ElevenLabs TTS
🏗️

Per-Tenant AI Pods

Each tenant gets an isolated OpenClaw instance. 50–100 pods per 4vCPU node. Fully reproducible from config templates — no snowflakes.

📡

Carrier-Grade Reliability

Idempotent message handling, per-tenant rate limiting, automatic failover. Broker deduplicates every event by messageId/callId. Built to never double-process.

🧠

MAaS Memory Persistence

Every pod is backed by ClawGuardian's Memory-as-a-Service. Your AI remembers every customer, every conversation — across calls, texts, and restarts.

⌨️

ClawTerm Terminal Access

Premium tenants get secure terminal access to their pod via Tailscale. Debug, inspect, or talk to your AI directly — like SSH to your own brain.

🔐

Runtime Secret Injection

Zero secrets baked into configs. All API keys injected from GCP Secret Manager at pod boot. Rotate keys once — all pods pick it up on restart.
